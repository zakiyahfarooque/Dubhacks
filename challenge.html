Sustainability Challenges

Daily Challenges

  • Share sustainability practices with others to raise awareness.
  • Recycle paper, plastic, glass, and other recyclable materials. Compost organic waste.
  • Conserve electricity by turning off lights and unplugging devices when not in use.
  • Reduce daily water usage by taking shorter showers and turning off taps when not in use.
  • Take public transportation or carpool to your destination.

Weekly Challenges

  • Support local farmers and buy locally-produced goods.
  • Participate in beach or park cleanup activities.
  • Reduce meat consumption and opt for plant-based meals.
  • Avoid single-use plastic products and use reusable alternatives.
  • Plant trees or participate in a local tree-planting event.

Monthly Challenges

  • Organize or participate in environmental awareness events in your community.
  • Volunteer for environmental organizations and initiatives.
  • Conserve water by fixing leaks and installing water-saving appliances.
  • Start a home garden and grow your own fruits and vegetables.
  • Calculate your carbon footprint and take steps to reduce it.
